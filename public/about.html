Who We Are

Clinomic Training Center for Clinical Research – Karnataka Branch is at the forefront of clinical research education. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practices by offering hands-on, industry-focused training, designed by experts.

  • 100% placement assistance for our trainees
  • Comprehensive internship programs
  • Innovative teaching methodologies
  • Strong industry network & collaborations

Message from the Director

Investing in People, Advancing Clinical Excellence.

In clinical research, the integrity and success of every trial depend on the knowledge and preparedness of the people behind it. That’s why this training center represents more than just infrastructure—it symbolizes our investment in people, in quality, and in the future of healthcare.

This center is where we build capability, instill a culture of ethics, and empower professionals to shape the future of clinical research.

Thank you to every individual involved in building, leading, and participating in this initiative. Your commitment is laying the foundation for safer studies, stronger science, and better patient outcomes.
